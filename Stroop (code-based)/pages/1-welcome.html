Stroop Task

Welcome to the Stroop experiment!

In this experiment, your task will be to identify the color of the word shown on the screen.
The word itself is immaterial — you can safely ignore it.

To indicate the color of the word, please use the keys r, g, b and o for red, green, blue and orange, respectively.
Please answer quickly, and as accurately as you can.

We'll start with some practice trials to get you warmed up.

Please press the space bar when you're ready.
